Frequently Asked Questions

Does MyPrescriptionPal collect my data?

No. The app runs entirely on your device. Your photos and prescription information are never sent to any server, stored externally, or shared with anyone — including us.

Why can't the app read my prescription label?

For best results, hold your phone steady in good lighting and make sure the full label is visible. Try taking the photo from a few different angles. The app works best with clearly printed text on flat surfaces.

What devices are supported?

MyPrescriptionPal requires an iPhone or iPad running iOS 16 or later.

Is the app free?

Yes, MyPrescriptionPal is completely free to use with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Overview

MyPrescriptionPal is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or health data. All information you enter into the app stays on your device.

Data Storage

All data, including your medication schedules, is stored locally on your device. None of your data is sent to any external server, cloud service, or third party. We have no ability to access, view, or retrieve your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, health information, usage analytics, or tracking data of any kind.

PDF Export

The app allows you to export your medication schedule as a PDF. This file is generated locally on your device. We do not receive a copy of any exported files.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.
